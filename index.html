About

I am a PhD student in Genomics at the School of Life Sciences, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, participating in the BGI-UCAS Joint Program under the supervision of Prof. Hao Zheng.

Prior to this, I obtained my Master of Engineering degree from China Agricultural University in 2023 and my Bachelor of Engineering degree from Northwest A&F University in 2020.

My current research focuses on investigating gut microbial diversity and host adaptation, using the honeybee as a model organism.
